in a park, an adult flies a kite into a cloud

who thinks it's a mushroom because a kid

on the ground looked up and thought it

looked like one and tried to take a bite,

then found that it was too far to reach for.


the cloud thought the kid had waved so it

tried to wave back and found that it was

just a mushroom, it didn't have any arms.


and then: BAM a kite straight to the cap.

those with ears noted the sound as, "less

of a clap, more of a poof," but it tore that

cloud apart.


the kid who saw a mushroom now sees a

heart, and knows it's too far, yet still falls

so hard, left with dreams of flying cars.


in the park, the adult who flew a kite all

afternoon checks the note he attached to

it to see if there was a response, but there

was none.
